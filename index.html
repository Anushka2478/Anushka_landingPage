About Me
DESIGN
THAT FEELS
AS GOOD
I'm Anushka Srivastava, a UI/UX Designer specialising in mobile app design, web & dashboard design, and end-to-end product design. I bring together deep UX research, systematic thinking, and refined visual sensibility.

My process is rooted in understanding real people — their friction, needs, and moments of delight. I use Figma, Framer, and AI-augmented workflows to move faster without losing depth.
